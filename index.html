CEFR B1 ESL Lesson Plan

Learning Objectives

  • Use time expressions correctly in speaking and writing.
  • Talk about and compare daily routines with a partner.
  • Read and understand a short text about time management.
  • Develop listening and speaking fluency through pair interviews.
